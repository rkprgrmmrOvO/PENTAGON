About Us


Who We Are




We are the Five Colors of Pentagon, composed of five (5) Bachelor of Multimedia Arts (BMMA) students who strive to achieve what we aim as we have different goals. The Pentagon's member names are Argenal, Camero, Diaz, Malano, Polinga. Our goal is to make art that'll inspire many people, entertain people as we, the BMMA students specialized in making different forms and variaties of art, not just ordinary art, an art made with pure passion with an unending fire that lits up our inspiration making pure masterpieces.




We're Different than the rest




We are honed in the art space, we experienced many different forms of art that covers our course of BMMA. We centralized in making original ideas and making that idea come to life.
